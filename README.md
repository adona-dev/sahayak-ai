# Sahayak AI

Sahayak AI is an intelligent assistant designed to simplify government form filling through conversation, voice interaction, and document recognition.

Instead of navigating complex government websites and forms, users can simply answer questions, upload their Aadhaar card, and the system automatically prepares the application.

---

## Problem

Many citizens struggle to apply for government schemes due to:

- Complex application forms
- Language barriers
- Lack of digital literacy
- Difficulty understanding required documents

As a result, millions of eligible citizens fail to access important services.

---

## Solution

Sahayak AI converts complicated government forms into a simple conversational experience.

Users interact with an AI assistant that collects information step-by-step and automatically fills the required application fields.

---

## Features

### Conversational Form Filling
The system asks users simple questions instead of showing long forms.

### Voice Input
Users can speak their answers using built-in speech recognition.

### Aadhaar OCR
Users upload their Aadhaar card and the system extracts important details using OCR.

### Multilingual Support
The assistant supports multiple languages:

- English
- Hindi
- Malayalam

### Field Explanation Assistant
Users can ask the meaning of form fields like income or address.

### Automatic Form Preview
All collected information is automatically structured into a form preview.

### Simple Submission Simulation
Users can submit the generated form in the prototype.

---

## How It Works

1. User starts conversation with Sahayak AI
2. The assistant asks required questions
3. User answers through typing or voice
4. Aadhaar card is uploaded for document extraction
5. The system fills the form automatically
6. User reviews and submits the application
